Life Thus Far

Ever since I ORD-ed in nov 2020, I have been working part time and learning more about computer
science. Since the beginning of this year, I have also started looking for internship opportunities
in this sector. It hasn't been easy trying to learn new programming languages and concepts

putting in a lot of effort to grasp the concepts while also juggling work and other commitments.

Life in Junior College

Looking back, 2 years of JC went by very quickly. Those 2 years were packed with many
